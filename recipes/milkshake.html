Chocolate Milkshake

A tasty frozen treat!

Ingredients:

  • 4 Scoops of Vanilla or Chocolate Ice Cream (Roughly 2 Cups)
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Chocolate Syrup

Directions:

  1. Place ice cream, milk and syrup in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth. You can garnish with sprinkles, whipped cream, oreos, etc.
  3. Enjoy!
