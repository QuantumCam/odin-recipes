Sweet Potato Fries

The perfect pairing with your blue cheeseburger!

Ingredients:

  • 2lbs Sweet Potatoes (Peeled)
  • 3 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 1/2 Tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Season of your choice: Paprika, Pumpkin, Garam Masala, Cajun or any other you prefer

Diirections:

  1. Preheat your oven to 450Â° and begin to cut your potatoes into 1/4 to 1/2 thick wedges.
  2. Toss the wedges with oil, salt and spice of your choice.
  3. Spread out the wedges onto a pan covered with a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for about 10 minutes and then move around with tongs before baking for another 10-15 minutes.
  5. Let them cool for about 5 minutes and then serve.
